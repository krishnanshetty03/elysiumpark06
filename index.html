Opportunity For Athletes

Elysium Calisthenics Park is India's first official calisthenics and street workout park. Founded in 2018, Elysium was setup with a goal to spread awareness about calisthenics (bodyweight training) and provide Indian athletes an opportunity to showcase their skills and talent.

Physical Training

Elysium Calisthenics Park is an outdoor fitness club that offers specialized training in bodyweight workouts. We provide fun and intense routines to help you get in shape, stay healthy and live better!

Mental Training

Elysium Calisthenics Park is a fitness club with an equally important mental wellness program. It takes holistic approach to health and helps people not only look good but feel good too.

Advantages

Course For Any Ages

Our goal is to help you build strength, burn fat, learn skills and improve health both physically and mentally. We offer park memberships and group training packages

Strength Training

Improve overall body and core strength with a major focus on the fundamentals of calisthenics

Freestyle and Flips

Learn the best dynamic and statics skills under the watchful eyes of our coaches

Muscle building

Build muscle through our specialised hypertrophy workout programs

Fat Loss

Achieve your dream physique by getting rid of unnecessary fat through bodyweight movements
